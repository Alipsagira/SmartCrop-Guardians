About SmartCrop Guardian

SmartCrop Guardian helps farmers in Nigeria detect crop diseases through image uploads. It uses machine learning to deliver instant diagnostics and advice—offline and in multiple languages.

Why It Matters

Crop loss affects food security. Many rural farmers lack expert diagnosis. SmartCrop Guardian bridges this gap with instant insights and support in Hausa and English.

App Features

  • 📷 Upload plant photos for instant diagnosis
  • 🧠 Get disease type, severity, and confidence level
  • 💡 View remedies and agronomic tips
  • 🌐 Works offline in rural areas
  • 🗣️ Supports English and Hausa languages

Impact Goals

We aim to reduce crop losses, boost resilience, and expand access to AI farming solutions across West Africa.
